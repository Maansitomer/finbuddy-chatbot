Source: RBI Update (July 2025)

Personal Loan: Cap of 14% p.a. interest rate for salaried individuals.

Home Loan: Must be linked to repo rate; banks to disclose margin above repo rate.

Business Loan: Priority lending norms for SMEs remain unchanged; collateral-free lending up to ₹50 lakh allowed under Mudra guidelines.

Education Loan: Moratorium period must be at least course duration + 6 months; collateral requirement waived for loans ≤ ₹7.5 lakh.
